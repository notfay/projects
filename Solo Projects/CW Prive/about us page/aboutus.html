About
Creating refined fashion that balances heritage, artistry, and responsibility.

Our Mission

At CW Privé, we envision a world where fashion is more than just style—it is an expression of timeless elegance, innovation, and responsibility. Our goal is to redefine modern luxury by blending classic craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics, ensuring that every creation is both exquisite and sustainable.

Our Vision

CW Privé is committed to creating high-end apparel, accessories, and footwear that exude sophistication and exclusivity. With meticulous attention to detail, the finest materials, and a dedication to sustainability, we craft fashion that inspires confidence while ensuring beauty and ethical craftsmanship go hand in hand.

Our Heritage & Craftsmanship

Founded by Christian Wijaya, CW Privé has become synonymous with refined luxury, earning recognition among style connoisseurs, celebrities, and trendsetters. From its early days to a globally renowned fashion house, the brand remains dedicated to blending heritage craftsmanship with modern innovation.

Every collection is a testament to this vision, preserving timeless artistry while redefining contemporary elegance. Archival imagery showcases our journey—honoring the past while shaping the future of fashion.

CW Privé

Our logo, simply "CW Privé" in an elegant and timeless typeface, represents the essence of our brand—minimalist yet impactful, modern yet steeped in tradition. It reflects our commitment to sophistication and understated luxury.

Flagship Presence

The headquarters of CW Privé stands as a testament to our vision of sustainable luxury. Housed in a LEED and BREEAM-certified skyscraper, our HQ is designed with eco-friendly materials, powered by renewable energy, and equipped with advanced energy-efficient systems to reduce its environmental impact. Every element, from the sustainable glass façade to the green rooftop gardens, reflects our commitment to responsible innovation.

Inside, the space fosters creativity and craftsmanship, with state-of-the-art ateliers, dedicated sustainability labs, and collaborative design studios that push the boundaries of modern fashion. As the heart of CW Privé, our headquarters is more than a building—it's a symbol of our dedication to shaping a greener, more refined future in luxury fashion.
